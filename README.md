### Hi there 👋! Business Analyst | Data Enthusiast | AI Learner

With 4+ years of experience across Google and Cognizant, I've spent my career at the intersection of data and business — writing complex SQL queries, building BI dashboards, automating workflows, and translating messy requirements into clean solutions.
What started as a deep dive into SQL and Excel has grown into a full analytics toolkit spanning Python (Pandas, NumPy), Power BI, Tableau, and now — AI. I'm currently channelling my business analyst mindset into building AI-powered projects that go beyond dashboards: automating analysis, generating insights, and exploring what's possible when you put LLMs to work on real business problems.
This repository is my open workbench — a place to share what I'm building, track what I'm learning, and connect with others who believe data (and now AI) can genuinely change how businesses think and operate.
💡 Always curious. Always building. Let's connect.
